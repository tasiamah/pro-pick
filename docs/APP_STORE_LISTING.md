## Description

Pro Pick brings AI-powered football match analysis to your iPhone. Browse upcoming
fixtures, view model predictions with confidence scores, compare live odds,
discover value bets, and track performance in the analytics dashboard.

**Features**

- Home dashboard with AI predictions hero and daily match picks
- Match list with search, status filters, and odds tiers
- Rich match detail with team form, insights, and odds movement
- Favorites for teams and competitions
- Analytics with ROI tracking and trend charts

Pro Pick is for entertainment only. It is not a gambling or betting service and
does not guarantee outcomes.
